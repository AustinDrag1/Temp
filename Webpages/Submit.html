After submitting a response, a rank between zero and five will be displayed. A high rank means there were more signs of depression detected. Additionally, a suggestion of how soon the patient should be seen by a professional will appear unless the response got a rank of zero.

If a response was found to show signs of severe depression, it will say so, instead of displaying a rank, and a suggestion for the patient to be seen immediately by a professional will appear.

Unless the response recieved a rank of zero, highlighting will be added to parts of the response that contributed to the evaluation
